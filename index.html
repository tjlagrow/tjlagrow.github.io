Hi, I’m TJ. I am an Instructor at Georgia Tech passionate about AI, neuroscience, and education at scale.

Whether I'm training large language models, teaching thousands of students in Georgia Tech's OMSCS program, or studying brain dynamics, I build things that bridge the gap between complex theory and real-world impact.

I serve as Instructor of Record for Georgia Tech’s graduate Machine Learning course (CS7641), leading 1,200+ students per term. I also co-founded a medical imaging startup and hold a PhD in ECE.
